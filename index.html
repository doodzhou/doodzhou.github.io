PROFILE

Zhou Peng is an Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology at Anhui University. He received his Ph.D degree in computer science from Hefei University of Technology, China. His research interests include data mining and machine learning, stream learning, feature selection, anomaly detection and explainable machine learning.
